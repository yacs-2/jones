Granmomommies 9 Step Brownies

  1. Prep Work

    • Wash GRUBBERS

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients

    1. In a large mixing bowl, add 2 cups of all-purpose flour, add 1 cup of brown sugar, and 1 cup of cocoa powder
    2. Add in 1 Tablespoon of baking powder. Also 1 tsp of baking soda.
    3. Set aside.

  3. Mix Wet Ingredients

    1. In a medium bowl, break two large eggs. Whisk until consistent.
    2. Add 1 tablespoon of salt to the egg mixture
    3. Pour half cup of milk into the wet mix.

  4. Combine Mixtures

    1. Place the large bowl with dry ingredients in a mixer on low setting
    2. Slowly pour in the wet ingredients until incorporated.
    3. Drop 8 tablespoons of room temp butter into the mixer. Consistency should be fluid but slow

  5. Pour

    1. Grease an 8x11 inch pan.
    2. Pour mix into pan. Use tongue to smooth it out.

  6. Bake

    1. Bake at 350 degrees F for 20 minutes

  7. Take out

    1. Use gloves to safely take out brownies until toothpick comes out at desired consistency

  8. Enjoy!!
